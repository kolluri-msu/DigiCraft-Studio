Interactive Stencil Maker

Instructions:

  • Click on the canvas once to place a starting point.
  • Click on the canavas again at a different place to form a line.
  • To use one of the Cut options, click one of them (Horizontal, Vertical, Slant, Curved) and repeat 1 and 2. This time instead of a line a cut is formed. For curved line trace the path by dragging the mouse instead of clicking at the end point. Remember not to click on the canvas again after pressing the curved cut button as this deactivates the mode.
  • To erase simply click on the erase button and click or drag the mouse on the canvas. To exit out of erase mode simply click on the erase button again. Remember to click the button again to deactivate the mode or the cursor will keep erasing.
  • To undo any errors use the undo button.

Instructions:

  • To create a maze use lines or the cuts.

Tangram

Instructions:

  • Use the seven shapes below to make the final shapes on the right.
  • You can move the shapes and rotate them but should not overlap them.
  • You must use all the shapes to make the final one.
  • When you click on the rotate button it remains active until you press it again. When active you can rotate any image on the paper.
  • You can drag the shapes on the paper.
  • Don't drag the pictures outside the paper.
  • If a shape overlaps another shape you might not be able to drag the one underneath. Move it aside first.
